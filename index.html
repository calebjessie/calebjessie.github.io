About me

I'm a self-taught UI/UX Designer and Front-End Developer based in Detroit. I’ve been designing since I was about sixteen and I’ve never felt better about my craft.

Designing is one of my greatest passions. When I’m designing I focus on the most important aspects; relevant content, structured hierarchy, ease of usability for the user, and purposeful design down to every pixel.

I know how users interact with the web and mobile devices and I design accordingly. Knowing user habits allows me to design interfaces that are easy to use and sit in the background unnoticed instead of getting in the way of the user.

To make things even better, I can implement the carefully crafted design for you as well. I always write my front-end code to be as semantic and reusable as possible; I’m a bit OCD when it comes to organized code.

So, do you wanna make a successful product or what? Lets create something that'll make a difference in the world. Hire me for your next project.
